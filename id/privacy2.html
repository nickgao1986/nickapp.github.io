Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

Short version: GlowCam processes photos on your device.

We do not collect, upload, sell, share, or track personal data from the app. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party analytics SDKs.

GlowCam is a screen fill-light and selfie app with beauty filters, cutout tools, photo editing, ID-style photo helpers, and social post layouts. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information for users in the United States, Europe, and other regions where the app is available.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data from the app. Your photos, videos, cutout masks, face-area processing results, edit history, app preferences, and usage counters are processed and stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them yourself.

2. Photos, Videos, and Editing Content

When you choose a photo or video, GlowCam uses it only to provide the feature you requested, such as fill-light capture, beauty retouching, background removal, image resizing, ID/profile photo composition, preview generation, or export.

3. Face Data and Biometric Information

Some features may use Apple on-device frameworks to temporarily detect faces, face rectangles, subject areas, segmentation masks, or similar visual regions. These temporary results help with beauty retouching, fill-light recommendations, ID/profile photo composition, subject positioning, and preview quality.

  • Face-related processing happens on device.
  • Face-related processing results are not uploaded to developer servers.
  • They are not used to identify you, sold, shared, or used for advertising.
  • They are discarded after the editing or preview session unless you choose to save the final edited image or video.

4. App Permissions

  • Camera: used for live preview, taking photos, and recording videos.
  • Microphone: used only when you record video with audio.
  • Photos: used when you select media from your library or save edited results to your library.

5. Local Storage

The app may store settings, preferences, feature counters, purchase state, and optional local editing history on your device using Apple-provided local storage.

6. In-App Purchases

GlowCam Pro is an optional one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase handled by Apple StoreKit. Apple processes payment, taxes, refunds, and transaction records.

7. Third-Party SDKs, Advertising, and Tracking

GlowCam does not use third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party login systems.
